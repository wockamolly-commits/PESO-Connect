Registration Step Structure

How should the required PESO fields be grouped into steps?

A

5 Steps (Compact)

Fewer screens, more fields per step. Faster to complete but denser.

1. Account (email, password)
2. Personal Info (name, DOB, sex, civil status, address, contact, disability)
3. Employment Status + Job Preference (status/type, preferred occupations, work location)
4. Education + Language (highest attainment, course, language proficiency)
5. Consent & Submit
B

7 Steps (Balanced)

One clear topic per step. Good balance of progress and focus.

1. Account Credentials
2. Personal Information (name, DOB, sex, civil status, religion)
3. Address & Contact (full address, phone, email)
4. Employment Status (employed/unemployed, type, OFW, 4Ps)
5. Job Preference (occupations, part/full-time, preferred locations)
6. Education & Language (attainment, course, language proficiency)
7. Consent & Submit
C

8 Steps (Granular)

Each section gets its own step. Feels lighter per screen but more clicks.

1. Account Credentials
2. Personal Information
3. Address & Contact
4. Employment Status
5. Job Preference
6. Education
7. Language Proficiency
8. Consent & Submit
Recommendation: Option B (7 Steps) — each step has a clear, single topic without being overwhelming. Employment Status and Job Preference are distinct enough to warrant separate steps, but Education + Language can share a step since language proficiency is just a quick checkbox grid.
